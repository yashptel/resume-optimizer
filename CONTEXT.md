# Domain Context

Glossary of domain terms used in ATS Beater. Update this inline as new terms are
resolved during conversations — don't batch.

## Resume Surfaces

There are two distinct resume data shapes in the system, and they must not be
confused.

### Profile Resume (`ResumeInfo`)
The user's master, structured resume. Stored once per profile, edited directly
by the user, and used as the source of truth when tailoring jobs. Schema lives
at `app/schemas/resume.py`.

### Custom Resume (`CustomResumeInfo`)
A job-specific tailored resume generated by AI from the Profile Resume + a job
description. One per job. Schema lives at `app/schemas/custom_resume.py`. This
is what the LaTeX template renders into the final PDF.

## Summary

A short professional pitch (typically 1-2 sentences) at the top of the resume.

There are two summaries with distinct semantics:

- **Profile Summary** (`ResumeInfo.summary`, `Optional[str]`) — user-authored
  baseline. Extracted verbatim from the uploaded PDF if present, otherwise null.
  The user can add, edit, or remove it from the profile UI. Never fabricated by
  the parser or the enhance flow.

- **Custom Summary** (`CustomResumeInfo.summary`, `Optional[str]`) — what
  appears on the tailored, job-specific PDF. Generated by Gemini Pro per job.
  When Profile Summary is set, it is used as the baseline and refined for the
  target JD. When Profile Summary is null, the AI may still generate a Custom
  Summary if it materially improves the resume.

A null Profile Summary means "the user hasn't written one" — it does **not**
mean "suppress summary on tailored resumes." Suppression of Custom Summary is
the AI's call based on whether one adds value.
